Bleed & Safe Zone Tool

Use the slider to understand how bleed works and ensure your design extends to the edges properly.

No Bleed With Bleed (Recommended)

What is bleed? Bleed is the area of your design that extends beyond the trim edge. This ensures no white edges appear when your print is trimmed to final size.

We recommend adding 0.125" (3mm) bleed on all sides of your document.
